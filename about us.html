ABOUT ACTION FOR SUSTAINABLE TRANSFORMATION « AST »

ACTION FOR SUSTAINABLE TRANSFORMATION (AST)
iAction for Sustainable Transformation is a Local NGO with operations in Rwanda and aimed at transforming vulnerable communities through a holistic approach focusing mainly on Providing quality education to students, Economic empowerment for mainly youth and women and Health programs working hand in hand with the communities on preventive measure to health challenges, forinstance Community health workers program, Nutrition and hygiene and Sanitation.
Vision of AST
A highly skilled, healthy and selfsustained community by 2030
Mission:
To promote Community Empowerment and wellbeing through quality Education, lifeskills development and community based health, nutrition, hygiene and sanitation services
Objectives:
i) Sensitize and support the community and learning institutions to achieve quality Education
ii) Build capacities and support the community for self Economic Empowerment and country development in general
iii) Promote community based health, nutrition, hygiene and sanitation Services Activities:
i) Sensitize and support the community for the establishment of Early Childhood Development (ECD) centers and spaces as well as other child friendly spaces
ii) Organize and conduct capacity building trainings for Teachers, school Managers and other Education stakeholders including community Education workers.
iii) Organize mentorship activities and study tours for teachers and Managers for leraning institutions.
iv) Support leaning institutions with Learning materials, equipment and infrastructure.
v) Organize and support exchange visits, study tours and conferences for Learning Institutions, Education practitioners to promote quality Education.
vi) Sensitize and support community and learning institutions for school enrollment and school retention of children.
vii) Support teachers and Educators’ initiatives in general for self economic development.
viii) Organize children and community reading and writing competitions
ix) Organize public debates for students
x) Organize co-curricula activities for students
xi) Organize and support adult literacy and numeracy activities.
xii) Sensitization, training, support and accompaniment of the community in terms of promotion of saving culture and good practices of financial management
xiii) Sensitization, training, support and accompaniment of the community in terms of income generating activities (IGAs) and job creation, focusing on off farm actitivies
xiv) Sensitization, training, support and accompaniment of the community in terms of vocational trainings, focusing on youth and women
xv) Sensitization, training, support and accompaniment of community in terms of promotion of hygiene and sanitation, nutrition, reproductive health as well as prevention of communicable and non communicable diseases, xvi) Sensitization, training, support and accompaniment of the Community in terms of good management and environmental Conservation.

AST believes that to achieve SDGs by 2030, there is need for concerted efforts and collaboration by different practitioners and working closely with the communities (beneficiaries for ownership) as key stakeholders, skills development through education at different levels and availing easy access to finance and financial literacy to the masses coupled with promoting the saving culture and lastly but not least, comeup with preventive measures for health challenges to reduce on huge expenditure on treatment.


We look forward to our collobaration and partnership for this Noble cause.
